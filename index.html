Portfolio (updated regularly)

Project 1: Recipe finder and Wine-Pairing application

The first collaborative assignment in the bootcamp. My team and I created a dynamic and interactive app that allows the user to search for recipes based off of an ingredient of their choosing. Something that stands out, is the ability to save the recipes to local storage, click on a new page, and this page allows you to find the wine that pairs the best with the dish. We had a great deal of fun learning through trial and error, and producing a practical and useful app.

Uses: HTML, CSS, Express, Moment, Mongoose

9-5 Work Day Planner

This is one of my favorite projects from the bootcamp because I use it on a daily basis, even though I don't even work a 9-5. The beauty of this app is that it boasts a dynamic use of html, CSS, and Javascript, to create a responsive app to plan your day out. The rows respond with color, and when the time of day passes they have a gray color, when it is the time they're red, and when you have time to plan they are blue. I invite you to go check it out for yourself! Another cool feature is being able to save your tasks locally, as a result when you refresh they're still going to be there.

Uses: HTML, CSS, Javascript

9-5 Work Day Planner Repo

Weather Calendar/Dashboard

A favorite bootcamp assignment of mine because of it's practicality and application to everyday life; I invite you to see for yourself the wonders that happen when you combine html, css, and js to make a fully-functioning Weather App! Simply type any city of your liking into the search bar, and the app will show you the weather in that city today, and for the next 5 days, complete with wind, humidity, and uv info as well.

Uses: HTML, CSS, jQuery, OpenWeather API
